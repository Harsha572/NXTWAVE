Favourite Places

Taj Mahal
Uttar Pradesh

Majestically located on the banks of Yamuna River, this wonder of the world was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Badrinath
Uttarakhand

Located at the base of the Himalayas, bordering Tibet, Badrinath is a famous pilgrimage town in Uttarakhand. It is a part of the Char Dham and Do Dham yatra undertaken by pious Hindus all over the world.

Kamakshi Amman Temple
Tamilnadu

Dedicated to Goddess Kamakshi, the temple is one of the 51 Shakti Peeths, and features an idol of Kamakshi Amman sitting in a yogic pose of Padmasana.

Baga Beach
Goa

Majestically located on the banks of Yamuna River, this wonder of the world was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.
