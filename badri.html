Badrinath

As a must-visit temple of the famous Char Dham Yatra, the Shri Badrinath Dham in Uttarakhand's Chamoli district is home to the Badri Narayana avatar of Lord Vishnu.

Overlooking the famous Hindu temple, the Nilkantha Mountain is the source of various glaciers supplying the major water bodies across the country and a popular trekking site for adventure enthusiasts.
